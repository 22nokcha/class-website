About Me

Hello, my name is Andrea Kim. I am currently a junior studying illustration. I plan to pursue a career in concept art/visual development for games or animated films. Although that is the ultimate goal, I am still unsure. Perhaps I will end up doing something else. No one knows.

Some of my hobbies include night runs/walks, light weightlifting, kicking, baking, small talk (sometimes), finding new bakeries/cafes, and trying new things. By "kicking," I just mean the action of it. I used to be a competitive martial arts athlete for many years so I have this lingering passion and enjoyment for kicking. I think it is so interesting how humans can manipulate their body, especially limbs, in different ways that can help them achieve different levels of power.

Speaking of interests, I recently have been dying over Peanuts comic strips and the cartoon. But specifically for Snoopy and Woodstock. Their close friendship is so cute and wholesome. I would say their friendship is the ideal of friendships.
